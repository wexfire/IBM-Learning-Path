Aspera Files Demonstration

Files enables fast, easy, and secure exchange of files and folders of any size between end users, even across separate organizations. Using Files, organizations can store and readily access files and folders in multiple cloud-based and on-premises storage systems. Sharing among users is as easy as browsing or dragging-and-dropping -- regardless of where the files are located -- freeing collaboration from traditional boundaries among colleagues in both local and remote locations.
